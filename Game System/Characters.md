# Experience

Adventuring is a quick way to improve yourself. You gain experience in regular intervals during your adventuring life.

When gaining experience, you make an [[Resources#Expenditure/Accumulation|accumulation check]] on some relevant level, depending on the situation. The [[Resources#Target|target]] of the accumulation check is usually the minimum, unless decided otherwise by the GM.

## Adventuring

### End of Scene

- Make [[Resources#Expenditure/Accumulation|accumulation]] checks for all [[Aspects|aspects]], [[Game System/Characters/Perks|perks]], [[languages]], [[movement modes]], [[Game System/Characters/Characteristics/Senses|senses]], and [[Familiarity|familiarities]] you used during the scene.
	- On a success, the following happen:
		- All other characters that took part in the scene, also gain a +1 increase on the same score, as long as their own level is lower than yours.
		- You gain a +1 bonus on the level of any other score, but it must be related to the one that advanced successfully just now.

### End of Quest

- You make an accumulation check on [[Experience Tier (ET)|ET]], and possibly [[Magic Aptitude (MA)|MA]] and [[Technology Knowledge (TK)|TK]].

### Downtime Activity

- You can select to improve any score as a [[Timekeeping#Downtime Activities|downtime activity]].
