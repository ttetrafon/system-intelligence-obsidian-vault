You keep track of your age in an abstract way by passing through age categories while your age ([[Resources|resource]]) accumulates.

Every # of [[Timekeeping#Natural|cycles]] you make an [accumulation check](onenote:Game%20System.one#Resources&section-id={F4B5F533-AED8-4749-B5F7-808512A72D86}&page-id={38F0484B-E8C7-4999-B7B9-924289A7357A}&object-id={3FD4263B-09E2-05B2-1788-3F757C99246F}&6A&base-path=https://d.docs.live.net/73fbda111a4ef6fc/Documents/System%20Intelligence). When your age reaches the indicated value for each age category, you pass to the next one.

|               | % (resource) | body checks | mind checks | soul checks |
| ------------- | ------------ | ----------- | ----------- | ----------- |
| infant        | 2            | dis. 3      | dis. 3      | dis. 4      |
| child         | 8            | dis. 2      | dis. 2      | dis. 3      |
| adolescent    | 15           | dis. 1      | dis. 1      | dis. 2      |
| young adult   | 20           | 0           | dis. 1      | dis. 1      |
| adult I       | 30           | 0           | 0           | 0           |
| adult II      | 40           | 0           | 0           | 0           |
| adult III     | 50           | 0           | 0           | 0           |
| adult IV      | 60           | dis. 1      | 0           | 0           |
| adult V       | 70           | dis. 1      | dis. 1      | 0           |
| venerable I   | 80           | dis. 2      | dis. 2      | dis. 1      |
| venerable II  | 90           | dis. 3      | dis. 3      | dis. 2      |
| venerable III | 100          | dis. 4      | dis. 4      | dis. 3      |

- {!important} If you advance from venerable III, you die from old age one random day during the following cycle.
