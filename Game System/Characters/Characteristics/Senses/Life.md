You can perceive the life force of creatures, even the [[Undead|undead]] and the [[Construct|constructed]] ones. You can use this vision mode to follow a creature's ❗psychic trail. You can perceive the life force of creatures behind all barriers, although [[Living|living things]], like foliage, block this type of vision.

Life sources appear as ghostly auras of different colours. Their colour determines their general type, blue for [[Living|living creatures]], orange for [[Construct|constructs]], and green for [[Undead|undead]].

You can identify different shades, with their intensity correlating directly to their life experience ([[Experience Tier (ET)|ET]]); the most vibrant ones belonging to the most experienced creatures.

The target's age desaturates a life aura to the point it looks washed-out despite still being vibrant.

Furthermore, this sense paints differently the living creature [[Descriptors (Tags)#Creature Types|types](../../../Gameplay/Descriptors%20(Tags).md) and [[Descriptors (Tags)#Creature Subtypes|subtypes](../../../Gameplay/Descriptors%20(Tags).md), meaning you can easily differentiate between them.

- ***Default Range***: ❗immediate
