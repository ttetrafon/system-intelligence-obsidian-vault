Your sense of smell can provide information about your environment and creatures, and includes your sense of taste.

Depends on chemicals dispersed in the air, and is not very precise unless you are very close to the emitter of the smell.

The sense of smell does not operate well (disadvantage 1-4) when the [[air is moving fast]] or the [[atmosphere is thin]], and cannot penetrate [[air-tight barriers]].

- ***Default Range***: [[Short|short]]
