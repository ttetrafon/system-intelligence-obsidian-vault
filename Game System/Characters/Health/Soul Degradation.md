Your pattern (soul) is being dispersed since it is not anchored to a physical form any more.

[10\*N] Every time this condition increases a level, you immediately lose 1d10 points on all of your [[Experience Tier (ET)|ET]], [[Mythic Tier (MT)|MT]], [[Magic Aptitude (MA)|MA]], [[Technology Knowledge (TK)|TK]], [[Aspects|aspect]], and [[Perks|perk]] values. This is a permanent reduction, and cannot be reversed, unless with extremely rare and powerful magic or technology.

- {!important} If your [endurance](onenote:#Aspects&section-id={EAB9ADFB-E6B8-4F73-ADBC-D294AE1A651D}&page-id={E86930DB-0FA8-4F4A-95A6-64D42CEC52FA}&object-id={9C43B4F3-4D62-2500-2CEB-2D2F1D06EDEE}&15&base-path=https://d.docs.live.net/73fbda111a4ef6fc/Documents/System%20Intelligence/Creatures.one), [willpower](onenote:#Aspects&section-id={EAB9ADFB-E6B8-4F73-ADBC-D294AE1A651D}&page-id={E86930DB-0FA8-4F4A-95A6-64D42CEC52FA}&object-id={9C43B4F3-4D62-2500-2CEB-2D2F1D06EDEE}&19&base-path=https://d.docs.live.net/73fbda111a4ef6fc/Documents/System%20Intelligence/Creatures.one), or [resolve](onenote:#Aspects&section-id={EAB9ADFB-E6B8-4F73-ADBC-D294AE1A651D}&page-id={E86930DB-0FA8-4F4A-95A6-64D42CEC52FA}&object-id={18DAA17F-E685-4405-3B42-9AA3F870D22D}&B&base-path=https://d.docs.live.net/73fbda111a4ef6fc/Documents/System%20Intelligence/Creatures.one) reaches zero due to this reduction, you immediately die and your soul cannot be recovered.
