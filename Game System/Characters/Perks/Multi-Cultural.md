## [[Ex|Ex]] IV
You have steeped yourself in various cultures that you can easily adapt and understand others. All your [[Social|social]] checks do not suffer any penalty when meeting a new culture; you are always considered fully [[../Characteristics/Familiarity|familiarised]] with it.

###### Requirements
- You must have studied 5 different cultures at least.
