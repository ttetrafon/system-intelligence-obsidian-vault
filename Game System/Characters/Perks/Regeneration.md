## [[Ex|Ex]]/[[Su|Su]]/[[Te|Te]] V/X ([[../../Gameplay/Descriptors (Tags)/Healing|healing]])
Your ability to recover from injuries is uncanny.
You can make one [[Attributes#Body|body]] and one [[Attributes#Mind|mind]] recovery check once per [[Timekeeping#Turn|turn]].
At **rank X*** you can attempt a recovery check for every injured [[Attributes|attribute]].

Regeneration is always accompanied by a specific [[Health#Damage Immunity, Resistance, & Vulnerability|vulnerability]]. If you take damage from your vulnerability, your regeneration is suppressed for that round.
