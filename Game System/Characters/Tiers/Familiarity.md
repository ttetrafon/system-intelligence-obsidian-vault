When interacting with something unknown to you, you may gain familiarity.

Familiarity operates as a [[Resources|resource]] and is used to determine (with a [[Chances Roll|chances roll]] if your action fails due to your limited knowledge/experience.

Each target for familiarity is individual, but may be related to other familiarity scores that share something similar.

Generally, a new familiarity score is assigned to anything totally new you encounter which is not normally covered by your aspects, knowledge, perks, etc. Its starting score is usually zero, but sometimes it may be a value determined by the situation or current circumstances.
