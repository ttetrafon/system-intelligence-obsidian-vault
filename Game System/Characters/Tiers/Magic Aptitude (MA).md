Describes how well versed you are in magic, both theorical and practical.

When attempting any check against magic or using any [[Su|supernatural ability]], you compare your MA with the target's [[Occult#Magic Level (ML)|ML]] and gain/suffer the difference as [[Game System/Gameplay/Descriptors (Tags)/bonuses & penalties/Experience|experience]] [[Gameplay#Bonuses & Penalties|bonus/penalty]] on your [[Gameplay#Checks|check]].

> Your current ML is 6, and you want to cast a ML 8 spell. This means you suffer disadvantage 2 to your Spellcraft check.

At the end of each [[Timekeeping#Quest|quest]], if you have interacted with magic extensively, and at least a few times the target magic was of higher ML than your MA, you may make an [[Resources#Expenditure/Accumulation|accumulation]] check to increase your MA.
