Describes your ascendance to mythical power.

Each point of difference between your own and an opponent's MT provides [[Gameplay#Advantage & Disadvantage|advantage/disadvantage]] on your checks.

> You are fighting a [[../../Checks & Dice/Descriptors/High Dragon|High Dragon]], which is a mythic creature (MT 2). You, being a typical, albeit experienced adventurer, you suffer disadvantage 2 on all your checks concerning the dragon.

- {!important} Note that the effects of  [[Experience Tier (ET)|ET]] and MT are independent, and they apply on the same checks in order.
