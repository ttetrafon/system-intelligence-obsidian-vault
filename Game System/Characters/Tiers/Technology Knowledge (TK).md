Describes your general technological aptitude and correlates directly to [[Technology#Technology Level (TL)|TL]].

When attempting any check against technology, you compare your TK with the target's [[Technology#Technology Level (TL)|TL]] and gain/suffer the difference as [[Game System/Gameplay/Descriptors (Tags)/bonuses & penalties/Experience|experience]] [[Gameplay#Bonuses & Penalties|bonus/penalty]] on your [[Gameplay#Checks|check]].

At the end of each adventure, if you have interacted with technology extensively, and at least a few of these instances where concerning technology of greater [[Technology#Technology Level (TL)|TL]] than your TK, you may make an [[Resources#Expenditure/Accumulation|accumulation]] check to increase your TK.
