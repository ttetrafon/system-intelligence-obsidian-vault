Traits are like [[Game System/Characters/Perks|perks]], but they are gained automatically as soon as their requirements are met.

You gain a rank in a trait automatically when you satisfy specific criteria, through magic/technology, or most often by satisfying the perk's specific requirements.

# All Traits

FROM #rules/characters/trait AND -"_templates" AND -#idea
