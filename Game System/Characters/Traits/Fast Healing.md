Your body can restore itself pretty easily.
You can make one [[../Attributes.md#Body|body]], [[../Attributes.md#Mind|mind]], or [[../Attributes.md#Soul|soul]] [[../Health.md#Recovery|recovery check]] once per turn.
At ***rank VIII*** you can make one [[../Attributes.md#Body|body]], one [[../Attributes.md#Mind|mind]], and one [[../Attributes.md#Soul|soul]] recovery check at the same time instead.
