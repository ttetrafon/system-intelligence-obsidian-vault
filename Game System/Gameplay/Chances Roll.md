In some cases you instead make a straightforward roll of d%. This simply succeeds if the result is below or equal to the score and fails if it is above.

Such cases are simple [[Game System/Gameplay/Descriptors (Tags)/bonuses & penalties/Luck|luck]] (*did I remember to pack lunch?, is there someone to ask for directions?, etc*) and [[Morality#Morality Checks|moral decision checks]] (*will I steal the gem put in the open when no-one is looking?*).
