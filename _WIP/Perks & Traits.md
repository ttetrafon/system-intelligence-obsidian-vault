## Evasion (Ex)

[Advancement]

You are fast moving and nimble, making it difficult for area effects to take hold of you.

- [30] Your mobility is capable of reducing incoming damage from area effects. This applies automatically, and comes before you attempt normal defensive checks.
- [90] You can attempt a movement check against incoming area effects. If successful, you move out of the way and suffer no damage at all from the attack.

## Flyby Attack (Ex)

[Advancement]

- [10] While flying in combat, you suffer only half damage from melee attacks.
- [40] You can use your flying movement aspect to negate incoming damage of any type, as you may move too fast for others to successfully damage you.
